About

The Debating Society is the official debating club of NIT Durgapur. Debsoc host some of the biggest debating events in the eastern circuit. Their aim is to promote the culture of debating on campus and help encourage building public speakers to overcome their fear and express their thoughts. At its core, this club is an ideological interest group that aims to promote the culture of debating on campus and remove the phobia associated with public speaking. They believe every voice matters and are dedicated to creating an accessible space for debaters, public speakers, and the like to be able to participate in constructive discussion and build new ideologiesthis.

Some of the events of Debsoc are :

  • Asian parliamentry debate
  • Oxford debate
  • Fictionary
  • Take Debait
  • Reverberate
  • What if

The club also organizes speaking workshops , exciting quizzes that require fast thinking and a thorough knowledge of every pop culture reference out there, and many other fun events.
